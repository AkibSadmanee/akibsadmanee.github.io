I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Information and Computer Sciences at the University of Hawaii at Manoa. I have avid interest in Natural Language Processing (NLP), a subfield of artificial intelligence that makes computers understand human languages. I work as a Graduate Research Assistant at Scalable Analytics and Informatics Lab (SAIL) under the supervision of Dr. Mahdi Belcaid.

My research interest lies in structured multi-document text summarization - a subfield of NLP that focuses on generating concise and coherent summaries from a collection of documents. During my Ph.D. I want to create a robust system to generate automated literature reviews from scientific research papers that are not confined by the limitations of the Large Language Models (LLMs). In addition, I am also interested in AI ethics in NLP and the biases embedded in the LLMs.
